Tools Tips/Things about Images
02 tank The majority of that air is nitrogen, coming in at about 78%, and the remainder is a mix of argon, carbon dioxide, neon, ect
BCD (Buoyancy compensator) Practice makes perfect
Regulator/s Your regulator is one of the most important pieces of your dive equipment. Remarkably, this piece of dive gear links together key components of your scuba gear.
submersible pressure gauge Essentially it measures the amount of remaining air pressure in a Scuba air tank.
Fins Fins improve swimming ability by increasing the surface area of our feet, allowing us to propel more easily through the water. They improve swimming speed by 30-40%.
Masks The solution to preventing mask squeeze is to remember to keep your nasal passageways open during descent by blowing small amounts of air through your nose every time you equalize your ears
Diving Comptures A dive computer takes depth and time information and applies it to a decompression model to track the dissolved nitrogen in your body during a dive.
Weights&belt You should make a weight check every time something changes. anything could affect your weight underwater.
